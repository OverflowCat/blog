About

Welcome! This is OverflowCat’s blog. The domain or site name, 新xīn世shì界jiè的de大dà門mén, lit. “the gate to a new world”, is a Mandarin slang that describes a brand new experience, such as learning a new language or crossdressing for the first time. By using this term as the site name, I hope I am writing something new and inspiring, from computer science to language learning. The blog system- will be changed in the near future + is under construction , and comments will be added. Nevertheless, I believe you should check my Mastodon account (@overflowcat@mastodon.social) in Fediverse if you are not following me.
